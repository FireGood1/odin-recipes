Mantu by Mirriam Seddiq

Go Back to Home

Description: The mantu is a thin dough that is filled with beef or lamb mixed with onions and spices. The steamed foodstuff then tops it off with an orange sauce made from chaka (thick creamy strained yogurt), giving this dish its unique taste!

Ingredients:

Meat Ingredients:

  • 1 Lb Ground Lamb (Can Use Beef)
  • 2 Cups Finely Diced Onions
  • 1 Spicy Green Chili
  • 2 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Tablespoon Ground Coriander
  • 1 Tablespoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper

Dough Ingredients:

  • 3 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 Tablespoons Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 1.25 Cups of Water (Customize to Your Flour and Weather Conditions)

Sauce Ingredients:

  • 1 Tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  • 1/4 Cup Finely Diced Onion
  • 1 8 oz Can Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon White Vinegar
  • 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tsp Black Pepper
  • Salt to Taste

Serving Ingredients:

  • Reheated Tomato Sauce
  • Yogurt Sauce
  • Cilantro (garnish)

Instructions:

For Dough:

  1. Step 1: Add flour, salt, and oil to the mixer bowl.
  2. Step 2: Start your mixer slowly and slowly add water. Don’t rush it. (Alternatively, you can knead by hand and slowly add water as you go)
  3. Step 3: Start trusting your eye. You have to let the water incorporate with your dry ingredients. You can eventually turn the mixer on high.
  4. Step 4: After a few minutes, you should notice the dough coming together. It will start to look like a stretchy piece of dough and will begin to grab your dough hook.
  5. Step 5: Want to knead by hand for about 6-7 minutes—mixer about 4
  6. Step 6: Even if using a mixer, I like to take the dough out and onto a flour surface and hand knead a few minutes to make sure the dough is firm

For Sauce

  1. Step 7: Add oil to pan
  2. Step 8: After the oil is heated, add onion. Sautee onion for about 2 minutes.
  3. Step 9: Add tomato sauce and spices. Stir and simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
  4. Step 10: Add vinegar, cook for 2 minutes, set aside.

For Filling

  1. Step 11: Mix meat ingredients from above until thoroughly combined. This will take several minutes.

Assembly of dumplings

  1. Step 12: Divide dough into four pieces.
  2. Step 13: Roll out one portion of dough until it is about almost see-through. It should be rolled into a rectangular shape. It should be pretty thin if you don’t mind a thicker or chewier dough, get it to where you can barely see the surface underneath.
  3. Step 14: As an alternative step to rolling it out entirely by hand and with a roller, you can use a pasta machine attachment.
  4. Step 15: Use a knife or pizza cutter and divide the rolled-out dough into equal-sized squares.
  5. Step 16: Put a scoop of filling into the center of one square. For the folding method, take opposite corners and pinch them together. This is the easiest way.
  6. Step 17: Place dumplings in the greased steamer basket. Cover steamer basket and let steam for approximately 30-35 minutes.
  7. Step 18: To serve, place the dumpling on a platter top with the reheated tomato sauce, yogurt sauce, and garnish with cilantro.
